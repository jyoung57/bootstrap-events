The Art Of Color

The Art of Color express the artist deep gratitude to two things, her craft and her tools. This work seems simple, yet extremely powerful by displaying a diversity of color and making each and every bristle from the paint brushes pop on the page. You can't help but notice the details in this work.

Artist - Rhondak
