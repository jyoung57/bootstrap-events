About Colorful Events

Colorful Events was created in 2023 by a developer that wanted to bring the world of color to the eyes of the public. While profit was not a motivator, the developer had to keep cost in mind in order for his project to continue to grow. So the art the you see is available for purchase to have in your own home. The artist are paid a portion of the proceeds and the rest goes to maintaining this site and charities. ALL IN THE NAME OF COLOR!!!
