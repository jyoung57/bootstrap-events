About Colorful Events

Colorful Events was created in 2023 by a developer that wanted to bring the world of color to the eyes of the public. Profit was not a motivator for this site, so the art the you see is not available for purchase. The artist are paid a portion of the proceeds from donations that are sent to the site and the rest goes to maintaining this site and charities. ALL IN THE NAME OF COLOR!!!
